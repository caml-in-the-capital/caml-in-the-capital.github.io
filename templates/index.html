About

Caml in the Capital is London's OCaml meetup group. We bring together developers, researchers, and enthusiasts to discuss OCaml development and share knowledge about the OCaml ecosystem.

Meetings

We host meetups once a quarter featuring talks, demos, and discussions about OCaml. The meetings are typically held at 18:30 UK time in Central London with both in-person and remote attendance options, though the time and venue may be adjusted to accommodate speakers.
